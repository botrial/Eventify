Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Question Answer
What payment methods do you accept? We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for payments.
Can I get a refund for my booking? Yes, refunds are available under certain conditions. Please check our refund policy for details.
Can I attend an event without registering? Registration is required for all events to ensure proper accommodations and resources.
Do you offer discounts? Yes, we offer discounts during special promotions or for early registrations. Keep an eye on our offers.
How do I know if an event is fully booked? You will see a notification on the event page if it is fully booked. We also provide a waiting list option.
